About Me

Astrophysicist-in-training with a special interest in machine learning and AI applications

I am currently a student at Santa Monica College. I've had the honor of researching gravitational waves with LIGO, where I learned how to navigate the ups and downs of research and carve my own path in the field. My work there sparked a passion for machine learning, leading me to UCLA's Break Through in Tech AI fellowship. Here I've gained hands-on experience with the end-to-end machine learning workflow, successfully building high performance models on real-world datasets. I'm also dedicated to closing the gender gap in STEM, which has inpsired my work with organizations dedicated to uplifiting minorities in the field. In my free time, I enjoy practicing Buddhist-based mindfulness, playing cozy games, and hanging out with my pug and three sassy torties.
